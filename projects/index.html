Work Experience Projects

AI Research for Monitored Growth - Sustainable Agronomy

Developed computer vision solutions for detection, segmentation, volume rendering, and point cloud analysis, resulting in two future publications and an open-source dataset of over 5,000 labeled images. This work was conducted in collaboration with the Agronomy Department at the University of Palermo, Sicily.

AI Research for AGN Galaxies - Astrophysics

Investigated relationships between features to predict the UV-to-X-ray emission ratio of Active Galactic Nucleus (AGN) galaxies, using large tabular dataset in a model-agnostic prediction approach. This project was carried out in collaboration with the Institute of Space Astrophysics and Cosmic Physics (INAF), Milan.

AI-Based Defect Detector for Watch Mechanisms
SOTA Review in Computer Vision

Developed an AI-based defect detector for a component of watch mechanisms, experimented augmentation with GANs. Involved working with multiple teams to collect data, agree on pipelines and technical decisions. Conducted a state-of-the-art review of computer vision applications in industry and built web tools to enhance data utilization and analytics.
